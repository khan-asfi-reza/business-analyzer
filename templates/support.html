Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

How do I create an account?

Click the "Dashboard" button in the navigation menu, then select "Create Account." Fill in your email, name, and password to get started. You can also start searching without an account.

Is Business Analyzer free to use?

Yes! Our basic features including search, company analysis, and watchlists are completely free. Premium features with advanced analytics may be available in the future.

What markets does Business Analyzer cover?

We cover both global markets (US, Europe, Asia) and regional markets (Bangladesh). You can search for public companies, private companies, commodities, and precious metals.

Using the Platform

How do I add items to my watchlist?

When viewing a company or asset detail page, click the "Add to Watchlist" button at the top right. You can manage your watchlist from the Watchlist page in the navigation menu.

What do the investment recommendations mean?

Our AI-powered system provides three types of recommendations: "Invest" (favorable opportunity), "Hold" (maintain current position), and "Don't Invest" (high risk or unfavorable conditions). These are educational suggestions, not financial advice.

How is the sentiment analysis calculated?

Our sentiment analysis uses natural language processing to analyze news articles, social media, and financial reports. The sentiment score (positive, neutral, or negative) is based on overall market perception and media coverage.

Account & Security

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot password?" on the login page. Enter your email address and we'll send you instructions to reset your password.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. We never sell your personal information. Read our Privacy Policy for details.

Can I delete my account?

Yes. Contact our support team at support@businessanalyzer.com to request account deletion. All your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

Investment Information

Does Business Analyzer provide financial advice?

No. Business Analyzer is an educational platform that provides information and analysis tools. We are not licensed financial advisors. Always consult with a qualified professional before making investment decisions.

How accurate is the market data?

We source data from reliable financial APIs and news providers. While we strive for accuracy, there may be delays or errors. Always verify critical information with official sources before making investment decisions.

Can I trade stocks through Business Analyzer?

No. Business Analyzer is an analysis and research platform only. We do not offer trading or brokerage services. You'll need to use a licensed broker to execute trades.
