Search & Discovery

How to Search

Use the search bar on the homepage or navigation menu to find companies and assets:

  1. Type the company name, stock ticker, or asset name
  2. Browse through search results
  3. Click on any result to view detailed analysis
  4. Use filters to narrow results by type or market

Search Tips:

  • • Use specific company names for best results (e.g., "Apple Inc." instead of just "Apple")
  • • Try stock tickers for quick access (e.g., "AAPL", "TSLA")
  • • Search by industry to discover similar companies
  • • Use the "Popular Searches" section for trending companies

Analysis Tools

Company Detail Page

Each company detail page provides comprehensive information:

  • Overview: Company description, founding date, market cap
  • Price Trend: Interactive chart showing historical price data
  • Financial Summary: Revenue, profit, and year-over-year changes
  • Sentiment Analysis: Market sentiment score and confidence level
  • AI Recommendation: Investment suggestion with risk assessment
  • Personal Notes: Add and save your own analysis notes

Reading the Charts

Our price trend charts show 6 months of historical data:

  • Hover over bars to see exact prices and dates
  • Blue bars indicate company stock prices
  • Yellow bars indicate commodity/dashboard/asset prices
  • Green percentage shows positive monthly change
  • Red percentage shows negative monthly change

Watchlist Management

Adding to Watchlist

Click the "Add to Watchlist" button on any company or asset detail page. The button will change to "In Watchlist" to confirm the item has been added.

Managing Your Watchlist

  • View all tracked items from the Watchlist page in navigation
  • See current prices and daily changes at a glance
  • Review investment recommendations for each item
  • Add and edit personal notes for each company/dashboard/asset
  • Remove items by clicking the trash icon

Understanding Recommendations

Important: Our recommendations are for educational purposes only and do not constitute financial advice. Always consult with a licensed financial advisor before making investment decisions.

Recommendation Types

Invest

Favorable conditions detected. Strong financial performance, positive sentiment, and good growth potential. Consider for portfolio addition after your own research.

Hold

Mixed signals or moderate conditions. Suitable for maintaining current position. Wait for clearer indicators before adding to position.

Don't Invest

Unfavorable conditions detected. High risk, negative sentiment, or declining fundamentals. Consider avoiding or reducing exposure.

Risk Levels

Low Risk:

Stable companies/assets with consistent performance

Medium Risk:

Moderate volatility with potential for growth or loss

High Risk:

Significant volatility and uncertainty

Investment Score

Our proprietary score (0-100) combines multiple factors including financial health, sentiment, market trends, and risk assessment. Higher scores indicate more favorable conditions.
